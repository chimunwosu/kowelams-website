Our Story

Excellence in Leather Craftsmanship

Kowelams International Ltd is a registered footwear and leather goods company founded in 2020 and headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

We specialise in the production of high-quality male and female footwear and leather belts, as well as the repair and maintenance of footwear, belts, and bags. We also offer professional training programmes for individuals who wish to develop skills in leather craftsmanship.

With a dedicated team of skilled professionals, we have built a reputation for quality, affordability, and excellent customer service since our establishment.
